What instruments do you teach?

I offer lessons in guitar, uke, bass, and piano/keyboard. Each lesson is personalized to your interests. Whether it be jazz bass or metal guitar, together we will get you where you want to be!

How long are the lessons?

Lessons are slated for either 30 or 60 minute sessions, depending on preferences and age. I recommend a consistent schedule (weekly/biweekly) to build skills effectively and maintain progress. Longer/shorter sessions available by request.
